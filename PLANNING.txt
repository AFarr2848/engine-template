- Resources and Instances
    - Resources are all owned by FeEngine with unique ptr
    - Instances are owned by some resource manager and referred to by index
    - Instances never call vulkan functions or resource stuff


- Bindless
    - Buffer 1 - UBO for global stuff (camera, light settings, etc)
    - Buffer 2 - Storange buffer for models and vertices and such. Device local
    - Buffer 3 - Instance buffer (storage also) for changing stuff. Host visible.
    - Buffer 4 - Staging buffer (transfer src) to move things to buffer 2.

    - Handles GLFW and such 

  - VulkanContext
    - Handles devices and validation

  - AssetManager 
    - Owns descriptor set, allocates memory, and uploads images
    - Compiles shaders 
    - Models too prolly 

  - World 
    - Contains all the information about stuff that exists
